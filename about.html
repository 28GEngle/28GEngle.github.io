Thing to write about #1 :)

Minecraft is a nice game. It is a game where you can use your imagination to make things like lava casts and creeper holes in peoples worlds, But theres more to it than that. you can add a bunch of cool mods to enhance your experiences like a gravestone mod to make sure your stuff doesn't despawn when you inevitably die to the monsters that lurk at night, or a backpack mod to make sure you can carry all of your stuff with you whereever you go if the vanilla inventory is just a little too small for your convenience.


My favorite activities to do in minecraft is to do random things. A few things I could do which would fall under the "random" category would be to entirely remove a chunk (including the bedrock), make multiple homes, or grief a village.
